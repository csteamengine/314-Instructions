Sleek

The following are instructions for how to create a sleek paper airplane. This design is faster than the traditional design and slightly more stable making it ideal for good distance and speed. It is slightly more difficult to make with a few more folds, however if you take your time, you can produce an excellent paper airplane. Follow the instructions below to learn how to make and launch this plane for optimal performance.

Supplies

8-1/2" X 11" Printer Paper

Optional

paperclip


Step 1

Start by folding the paper in half lengthwise and open it out again. Be sure to line to corners up as close as you can and make a clean crisp fold.

Step 2

Fold the right and left sides in to line up evenly with the center-fold. Once completed, open the paper up so it lays flat again.
